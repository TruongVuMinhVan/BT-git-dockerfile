Key Features

💊 Smart Medication Management

  • Add, edit, delete medications with detailed info
  • Smart reminders – push notifications on time
  • Track medication history with daily/weekly/monthly schedules
  • Sync between web and mobile platforms

📈 Health Monitoring

  • 5 vital signs: Blood pressure, Heart rate, Blood sugar, Weight, Temperature
  • Smart charts with trend analysis over time
  • Abnormal value alerts and detailed history (GMT+7)

🤖 AI Medical Chat (Gemini)

  • Natural Vietnamese chat with Gemini AI – 24/7 health consultation
  • Drug guidance, symptom assessment, nutrition advice
  • Personal health data analysis with smart suggestions

🔬 AI Skin Disease Prediction

  • Upload image → AI auto-analyzes in 2-3 seconds
  • 94.1% accuracy with Ensemble Learning (ResNet50 + ViT)
  • 11 disease types: Melanoma, Eczema, Psoriasis, etc.
  • Top-3 predictions with confidence scores

📅 Appointment Management

  • Schedule appointments with doctors, clinics
  • Reminders: 15 min, 1 hour, 1 day before appointment
  • Location details, notes, recurring appointments

Training Details

  • Dataset: 24,086 high-quality dermatology images – 11 disease classes
  • Techniques: Transfer Learning, Mixup/CutMix, Label Smoothing
  • Platform: Google Colab with GPU Tesla T4/V100
  • Framework: PyTorch + timm + torchvision
  • Ensemble: ResNet50 (40%) + ViT (60%) weighted prediction
